Chronic Kidney Disease — Overview for Seniors

Chronic Kidney Disease affects approximately 37 million Americans. Seniors with this condition may qualify for additional benefits and support services at the federal and state levels.

How Many Seniors Are Affected

37 million Americans

Medicare Coverage for Chronic Kidney Disease

Medicare covers medically necessary treatments for Chronic Kidney Disease, including doctor visits, hospital stays, prescription drugs (Part D), and some home health services.

Medicaid Coverage for Chronic Kidney Disease

Medicaid may cover treatments, medications, home care, and long-term care services for seniors with Chronic Kidney Disease. Coverage varies by state.

VA Benefits for Veterans with Chronic Kidney Disease

Veterans with Chronic Kidney Disease may qualify for VA disability compensation, health care, Aid & Attendance, and other benefits.
