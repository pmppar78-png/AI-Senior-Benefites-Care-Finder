Dementia (Non-Alzheimer's) — Overview for Seniors

Dementia (Non-Alzheimer's) affects approximately 5 million Americans. Seniors with this condition may qualify for additional benefits and support services at the federal and state levels.

How Many Seniors Are Affected

5 million Americans

Medicare Coverage for Dementia (Non-Alzheimer's)

Medicare covers medically necessary treatments for Dementia (Non-Alzheimer's), including doctor visits, hospital stays, prescription drugs (Part D), and some home health services.

Medicaid Coverage for Dementia (Non-Alzheimer's)

Medicaid may cover treatments, medications, home care, and long-term care services for seniors with Dementia (Non-Alzheimer's). Coverage varies by state.

VA Benefits for Veterans with Dementia (Non-Alzheimer's)

Veterans with Dementia (Non-Alzheimer's) may qualify for VA disability compensation, health care, Aid & Attendance, and other benefits.
