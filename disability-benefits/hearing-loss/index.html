Hearing Loss — Overview for Seniors

Hearing Loss affects approximately 48 million Americans. Seniors with this condition may qualify for additional benefits and support services at the federal and state levels.

How Many Seniors Are Affected

48 million Americans

Medicare Coverage for Hearing Loss

Medicare covers medically necessary treatments for Hearing Loss, including doctor visits, hospital stays, prescription drugs (Part D), and some home health services.

Medicaid Coverage for Hearing Loss

Medicaid may cover treatments, medications, home care, and long-term care services for seniors with Hearing Loss. Coverage varies by state.

VA Benefits for Veterans with Hearing Loss

Veterans with Hearing Loss may qualify for VA disability compensation, health care, Aid & Attendance, and other benefits.
