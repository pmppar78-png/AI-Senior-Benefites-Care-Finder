{{#if medicareCoverage}}

{{{medicareCoverage}}}

Medicare provides coverage for diagnosis, treatment, and ongoing management of {{condition}}. This may include doctor visits, hospital stays, lab tests, medications (through Part D), durable medical equipment, home health care, and skilled nursing facility care. Coverage specifics depend on your Medicare plan type.
