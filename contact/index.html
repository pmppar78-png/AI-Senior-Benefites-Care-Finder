Get in Touch

For general questions about our guides, feedback on our content, suggestions for new topics, or any other inquiries, please reach out by email:

Report an Error or Correction

Accuracy is at the core of everything we do. If you spot information in any of our guides that you believe is inaccurate, outdated, or misleading, we want to know about it.

Corrections email: corrections@seniorbenefitsfinder.com

When reporting an error, please include:

  • Page URL: The web address of the page containing the information you believe is incorrect.
  • Specific information: The exact claim, figure, or statement you believe is inaccurate.
  • Correct information: If you know the correct information, please share it along with a source (such as a government website or official document).

We take every correction report seriously and investigate all submissions against our primary sources. Verified errors are corrected promptly. You can learn more about our approach to accuracy in our fact-checking policy and editorial policy.

For Seniors & Families

If you are a senior or family member looking for help understanding benefits, we have several resources that may assist you:

  • State guides: Our guides cover Medicare, Medicaid, assisted living costs, home care, Social Security, veterans benefits, and more — all broken down by state. Start by exploring the topic that is most relevant to your situation from our home page.
  • AI chat tool: Our AI guide can help you explore general questions about benefits and care options. It is a good starting point for understanding what programs may be available to you.
  • Comparison tools: Our comparison tools let you evaluate different care and benefit options side by side.

While we cannot provide personalized advice about your specific benefits, eligibility, or enrollment, our guides can help you understand the landscape and prepare informed questions for licensed professionals who can assess your individual situation.

Important Note

Senior Benefits Care Finder is an informational resource. We are unable to provide personal advice about your individual benefits, eligibility, enrollment, or care decisions. Please understand that:

  • We cannot determine whether you qualify for specific programs.
  • We cannot help you enroll in Medicare, Medicaid, Social Security, or any other program.
  • We cannot recommend specific insurance plans, care providers, or financial products.
  • We cannot provide legal, financial, medical, or tax advice for your situation.

For personalized assistance, please work with licensed professionals who can evaluate your specific circumstances. This includes licensed insurance agents for Medicare questions, elder law attorneys for legal matters, certified financial planners for financial decisions, and your healthcare providers for medical questions.

Response Times

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 business hours. During periods of high volume — particularly around Medicare Open Enrollment (October through December) and other major program enrollment periods — response times may be slightly longer.

Correction reports are prioritized and investigated as quickly as possible. If a reported error is verified, it will be corrected on the affected page regardless of our normal content update cycle.
