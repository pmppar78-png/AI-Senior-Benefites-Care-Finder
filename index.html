Safety & scam awareness

Seniors are often targeted by aggressive marketing and scams. The AI can help you practice what to say when you feel pressured, and remind you of red flags to watch for. It will often suggest slowing down, writing things down, and double‑checking offers with trusted family members or advisors.

You should never share full Social Security numbers, bank information, or credit card numbers in the chat. Those details are for secure conversations only with official entities you trust.
