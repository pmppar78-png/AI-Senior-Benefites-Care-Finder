Medicaid Eligibility by State (2026)

Medicaid provides essential healthcare coverage for low-income seniors, people with disabilities, and families. Eligibility rules, income limits, and available programs vary significantly by state. Our guides cover each state's Medicaid program including home and community-based services (HCBS) waivers, spend-down rules, application processes, and dual eligibility with Medicare.
