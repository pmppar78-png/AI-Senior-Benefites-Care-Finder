Transparency Statement

We believe you deserve to know how this site is funded and how we keep our editorial content independent. This page explains our business model, advertising practices, and the principles that guide everything we publish.

Updated: March 9, 2026 Policy
Important: This page is for informational purposes only. It does not constitute financial, legal, tax, medical, or insurance advice. Always confirm details with official program representatives and licensed professionals before making decisions. Data may change -- verify current figures with official sources.

How This Site Is Funded

Senior Benefits Care Finder is funded through advertising and affiliate partnerships. This means we may earn revenue when:

  • Display advertisements are shown on our pages
  • Readers click on affiliate links that connect them to third-party services or products
  • Advertising partners sponsor content placements on the site

This revenue supports the ongoing research, writing, fact-checking, and maintenance of the free informational guides we publish. Without advertising and affiliate revenue, we would not be able to provide this resource at no cost to readers.

Editorial Independence From Commercial Relationships

Our editorial content is created independently of our advertising and affiliate relationships. This is a foundational principle of Senior Benefits Care Finder:

  • Advertisers and affiliate partners do not influence the content, conclusions, or recommendations in our guides
  • Our research methodology and fact-checking process are applied consistently regardless of any commercial relationship
  • We do not allow advertisers to review, approve, or edit our editorial content before publication
  • The presence or absence of an advertising relationship with a company does not affect whether or how we cover that company's products or services

Our editorial team operates independently from our business and advertising teams. Editorial decisions are based on what is most useful and accurate for our readers, not on commercial considerations.

Affiliate Link Disclosure

When our content includes affiliate links -- links that may result in compensation to Senior Benefits Care Finder if a reader clicks through or takes an action -- we disclose this clearly. Our affiliate disclosure practices include:

  • Pages containing affiliate links include a disclosure statement informing readers of the relationship
  • Affiliate relationships do not affect our editorial coverage, ratings, or the order in which information is presented
  • We only partner with companies and services that are relevant to our audience of seniors and caregivers

We believe that transparency about these relationships is essential to maintaining your trust.

No Compensation From Government Agencies or Programs

Senior Benefits Care Finder does not receive compensation, funding, or any form of payment from government agencies or the benefits programs we cover. This includes:

  • We receive no payments from the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS), the Social Security Administration (SSA), the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA), or any state agency
  • We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any government program
  • Our coverage of government programs is based solely on publicly available information and our independent research

This independence from government funding ensures that our content is guided by the interests of our readers, not by any institutional relationship.

We Do Not Sell, Enroll, or Recommend Specific Plans

Senior Benefits Care Finder is an informational resource, not an insurance agency, enrollment service, or financial advisor. We want to be clear about what we do and do not do:

  • We do not sell insurance policies, Medicare plans, or any financial products
  • We do not enroll readers in any benefits program
  • We do not recommend specific insurance plans, financial products, or service providers in our editorial content
  • We do provide educational information to help readers understand their options and make informed decisions with the help of licensed professionals

How Advertising Is Labeled and Separated

We take care to ensure that advertising content is clearly distinguishable from our editorial content:

  • Display advertisements are visually distinct from editorial content
  • Sponsored content, if any, is clearly labeled as such
  • Affiliate links are disclosed on pages where they appear
  • Our editorial guides are never disguised as advertisements, and advertisements are never disguised as editorial content

If you are ever unsure whether something on our site is editorial content or advertising, please contact us and we will clarify.

Our Commitment to Readers' Interests

Above all, Senior Benefits Care Finder is built to serve seniors and their caregivers. Our commitment to putting readers first means:

  • We prioritize accuracy and usefulness over revenue generation in all editorial decisions
  • We provide information that helps readers understand their options, even when there is no commercial benefit to us
  • We encourage readers to verify information with official sources and consult licensed professionals before making decisions
  • We maintain clear disclaimers reminding readers that our content is informational, not professional advice

Your trust is the foundation of our work. We are committed to earning and maintaining it through transparency, accuracy, and an unwavering focus on your interests.
